WordPress plugin

To install instant.page on your WordPress site:

  • Go to your dashboard > Plugins > Add New
  • Search for “instant.page” in the search bar at the top right
  • Click on “Install Now”
  • Click on “Activate”
Your blog’s pages should now be instant!

You may alternatively download it from the WordPress plugin directory.

Adding <body> attributes

If you want to add attributes to the <body> as detailed in Pages not preloaded go to your dashboard > Appearance > Theme Editor > Theme Header (header.php) on the right. You’ll be able to add attributes to the <body> there.
