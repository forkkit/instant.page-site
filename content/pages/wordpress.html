WordPress plugin

To install instant.page on your WordPress site, go to your dashboard > Plugins > Add New, and search for “instant.page” with the search bar at the top right, then click on “Install Now” and then “Activate”. Your blog’s pages should now be instant!

Adding <body> attributes

If you want to add attributes to the <body> as detailed in Pages not preloaded go to your dashboard > Appearance > Theme Editor > Theme Header (header.php) on the right. You’ll be able to add attributes to the <body> there.
